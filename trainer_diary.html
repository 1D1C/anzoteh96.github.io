Anzo's Mathematics

IMO 2016 Trainings

I was invited by Mr. Suhaimi Ramly to deliver geometry lectures in the IMO 2016 training camps in January, March (pre-APMO camp) and April.

Lectures Given

I gave a geometry lecture in each of the three training camps stated above, namely:

  • January: Trigonometric bashing in solving geometry problems (with the fundamentals exactly the same as here).
  • March: Projective geometry (poles and polars, harmonic bundles, homothety, Monge's theorem, Monge d'Alembert theorem).
  • April: Three geometry theorems (Simson's theorem, Casey's theorem, and Sawayama Thebault's theorem).

Problem-solving session conducted.

In addition to the geometry lectures I have given, I have also conducted problem solving sessions based on the following projects I have done in the 2012 International Mathematics Tournament of Towns Summer Conference (that I attended together with Justin) :

  • Amazing properties of binomial coefficients
  • Fair cake division
I talked about these in the March and April training camps, respectively.
